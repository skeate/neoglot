Social Conlanging

neoglot is a tool to help you create, update, and share your constructed languages. Some features it provides (or will, eventually):

  • Change tracking
  • Pattern-based word generation
  • Dictionaries
  • Conscript creator
  • Forum for posts in conlang

What is a conlang?

A conlang (short for constructed language) is a language created by a person or group of people for some purpose, such as easing communication (e.g. Esperanto) or for a fictional world (e.g. Tolkein's Quenya). Many exist, including some you're probably familiar with, at least by name. For more information, see this Wikipedia article.
