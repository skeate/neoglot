About

I started working on Neoglot because I noticed there was a lack of good tools out there for conlangers, with most people, at best, making their own sites to share them&em;at worst, never sharing them at all. I figured I could help the community a bit by making this site.

neoglot is an Open Source project. If you run into any issues, please post them on Github, post them in this reddit thread, or contact me directly. Ultimately it will wind up on Github anyway, so that is the preferred method.

While the server/bandwidth requirements for this site will not likely ever be very high, they are not free. Donations are greatly appreciated.
